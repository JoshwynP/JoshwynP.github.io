Projects

Sw1ft Shot

"Sw1ft Shot" is an exhilarating speed-run focused target shooting game developed by me from its inception to its current stage. The game features multiple levels with varying difficulty, themes, and obstacles to overcome. Leveraging the power of Unreal Engine 5, I utilized its Blueprint Visual Scripting system alongside custom C++ code to design and implement intricate gameplay mechanics, including dynamic target and obstacle movement algorithms, scoring systems, and engaging character interactions. Additionally, I meticulously crafted captivating levels and environments within "Sw1ft Shot," employing industry-standard tools and techniques to create diverse landscapes and challenging obstacles. To ensure an immersive gaming experience, I implemented intuitive user interfaces and visually striking HUD elements, applying principles of UI/UX design for seamless navigation and enhanced player immersion.

Ice Cube Splash

Skills:

  • 3D modeling
  • Animation
  • Liquid simulation
  • Lighting & shading
  • Rendering within Blender

In this Blender project I crafted four realistic ice cubes falling into red water. Throughout the project, I delved into various aspects of 3D modeling, refining my skills in creating detailed and lifelike objects. From shaping the ice cubes to designing the glass cup, I honed my ability to capture realistic textures and proportions. Moreover, I explored animation techniques to bring the ice cubes to life as they descended into the liquid. This involved working with keyframe animations and timing to achieve natural and fluid motion. Additionally, I utilized blender's own liquid simulation, experimenting with parameters to accurately depict the behavior of the liquid as the ice cubes interacted with it. Furthermore, I gained experience in lighting, shading, and rendering to enhance the visual quality of the scene. Adjusting lighting setups and applying appropriate materials to the objects allowed me to achieve a more realistic and visually appealing result.
